Random Rainbow Lab

RANDOM RAINBOW

Random Rainbow Lab is an experimental division of Random Rainbow, a cyber art project designed to connect queer video art through random experiences.

While the main Random Rainbow platform focuses on video art curation and artist connections, the Lab explores experimental interfaces and interactive experiences. The Hand Controller is our first cyberdelic experiment, transforming hand movements into mesmerizing visual effects.

Built with Processing, this project leverages the power of creative coding to deliver real-time visual effects and interactive art experiences. As an open source project, we believe in the power of community-driven development and welcome contributions from artists and developers alike.
