🎵 Hand Music Controls

Right Hand:
• Thumb: Point direction to change distortion effect
• Index: Point direction to change musical scale
• Middle: Point direction to change instrument
• Ring: Point direction to change mode (melody/chord/rhythm)
• Pinky: Point direction to control distortion speed
• Pinch (thumb to index) to trigger notes
Left Hand:
• Thumb: Extend to control distortion intensity
• Index: Extend to control reverb amount
• Middle: Extend to control delay amount
• Ring: Move up/down to control master volume
• Pinky: Extend to start/stop recording
• Move wrist to control overall volume/effects
Finger Controls:
• Point right: Major scale, Synth, Melody mode, Larsen effect
• Point down: Minor scale, Piano, Chord mode, Mirror effect
• Point left: Blues scale, Bass, Rhythm mode, Wormhole effect
• Point up: Pentatonic scale, Drums, Random mode, Random effect
